👋 About Emma

I'm an independent support worker based in Wollongong with over five years of experience supporting NDIS participants and aged care clients. I take a calm, patient, and person-centred approach — my goal is always to help people feel comfortable, respected, and empowered in their daily lives.

I love getting outdoors and staying active, so community access shifts are a real highlight for me. Whether it's a trip to the beach, a coffee run, or just getting out of the house for a walk, I'm all about making those moments enjoyable. I also have experience with personal care, meal preparation, and helping people build independence with daily routines.

Outside of work, I'm big on hiking, cooking (I make a mean pasta), and I play in a local social netball team. I find that sharing interests with the people I support makes a real difference — it turns a shift into genuine time spent together.
